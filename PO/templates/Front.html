What is portfolio optimization and why should i care?

Portfolio optimization is the process of selecting the best combination of assets (such as stocks, bonds, real estate, or other investments) to maximize returns while minimizing risk based on your financial goals and risk tolerance. It relies on mathematical models and statistical techniques, such as Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT) by Harry Markowitz, to construct an efficient portfolio.
